About Me

What this is all about, my portfolio, resume, and more

Hi!

Welcome to a my first steps at a passion project; I hope you've enjoyed your time here. My name is Kyle Xu, I'm an American born Chinese whose parents immigrated after various issues that arose back in China during the 80s and 90s. I moved from St. Louis, Missouri to Southern California at age 11, and have loved it here ever since. I'm currently a student studying at UCLA, and work on various programming and web development projects on the side.

I love reading, hence the rest of the website, but my travels abroad have also convinced me of the value of stepping out into the "real" world. In "Water", I hope to convey things I've learned both amongst the pages and places once foreign to me. I owe much of what I know to these experiences. 

Much of my inspiration for this website comes from the world we live in. 2020 has been an absolutely crazy year, and the world around as well as within my own mind has been utter chaos. To give life to the machinations of my brain this year, I created "Water". Giving form to my thoughts has helped me process the things that have happened, both to me and the world.
